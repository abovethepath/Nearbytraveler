2) Important class fixes (kills scrollbars & overlap)

Avatar scrollbars are from an inner container using overflow-auto or mismatched sizes. The wrapper above uses overflow-hidden + ring-4 so you never see a scrollbar.

Ensure your page shell has no horizontal overflow:

<html class="overflow-x-hidden">
<body class="overflow-x-hidden">


and in your app layout:

<main className="min-h-screen w-full overflow-x-clip">{children}</main>


Don‚Äôt set fixed heights on the gradient header (e.g., remove h-[180px]/max-h-*). Let content define height; that‚Äôs what prevents the Edit button from climbing over text.

Anywhere you render pills/badges, wrap with a container that wraps and doesn‚Äôt scroll:

<div className="flex flex-wrap gap-2 items-center overflow-visible max-w-full">
  {/* <Badge>‚Ä¶ */}
</div>
