## ğŸŒŸ Core Philosophy

**95/5 Rule**: People spend 95% of their life as LOCALS, only 5% traveling

- All users sign up as LOCALS first to establish hometown knowledge
- Current travelers are locals temporarily away from home
- LOCAL-first signup approach builds authentic community connections
- Bucket system organizes users by current location and hometown
