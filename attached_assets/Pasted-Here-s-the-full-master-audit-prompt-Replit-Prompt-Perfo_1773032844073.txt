"Discover People" title must never be truncated — move to its own line above the filter buttons

Traveler cards in the feed must be fully responsive with no horizontal bleed

Audit the entire page for horizontal overflow — nothing should bleed past the viewport

Consistent padding of at least 16px on left and right margins throughout the page
