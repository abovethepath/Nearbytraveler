That means inside the About/Profile card you opened a <div> and never closed it before </CardContent>. Vite expects </div> at 3681 and crashes the whole page.

Do this exactly

Open client/src/pages/profile.tsx.

Jump to around lines 3640–3695.

Find the <CardContent> that ends at line 3681. Make sure everything inside it is wrapped and closed like this:

<Card>
  <CardHeader>…</CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    <div className="space-y-4">
      {/* your blocks go here */}
      {/* e.g. Business name / Bio / From & Age rows */}
    </div>  {/* ← THIS closing div must exist */}
  </CardContent>
</Card>


Most likely you have one of these patterns inside that block:

{condition && ( <div> ... )} but the inner <div> wasn’t closed, or

A closing )</div> pair is missing a </div> before </CardContent>.

A safe drop-in replacement (paste over your current About card)

If you want a guaranteed-balanced section, replace your current About Section (from // About Section comment down to the closing </Card>) with this:

{/* About Section */}
<Card className="mt-6 overflow-visible">
  <CardHeader className="pb-2">
    <div className="flex flex-wrap items-start justify-between gap-3">
      <h3 className="text-lg font-semibold min-w-0 break-words">
        About {user?.userType === "business"
          ? (user?.businessName || user?.name || user?.username)
          : (user?.username || "User")}
      </h3>

      {isOwnProfile && (
        <Button size="sm" variant="secondary" className="shrink-0" onClick={() => setIsEditMode(true)}>
          <Edit2 className="h-4 w-4 mr-2" /> Edit Bio
        </Button>
      )}
    </div>
  </CardHeader>

  <CardContent>
    <div className="space-y-4">
      {user?.userType === "business" && (
        <div className="p-4 bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-50 to-orange-50 dark:from-blue-900/20 dark:to-orange-900/20 rounded-lg border border-blue-200 dark:border-blue-700">
          <div className="flex items-center justify-between mb-2">
            <h4 className="font-semibold text-blue-800 dark:text-blue-200">Business Name</h4>
            {isOwnProfile && (
              <Button size="sm" variant="outline" onClick={() => setIsEditMode(true)}
                className="bg-gradient-to-r from-blue-500 to-orange-500 text-white border-0 hover:from-blue-600 hover:to-orange-600">
                <Edit2 className="w-3 h-3" />
              </Button>
            )}
          </div>
          <p className="text-lg font-bold text-blue-900 dark:text-blue-100">
            {user?.businessName || "Business name not set"}
          </p>
        </div>
      )}

      <div>
        <p className="text-muted-foreground whitespace-pre-wrap break-words">
          {user?.userType === "business"
            ? (user?.businessDescription || user?.bio || "No business description available yet.")
            : (user?.bio || "No bio available yet.")}
        </p>
      </div>

      <div className="text-sm text-muted-foreground flex flex-wrap items-center gap-x-3 gap-y-1">
        {([user?.hometownCity, user?.hometownState, user?.hometownCountry].filter(Boolean).length > 0) && (
          <span className="whitespace-normal break-words">
            From:{" "}
            <span className="font-medium">
              {[user?.hometownCity, user?.hometownState, user?.hometownCountry].filter(Boolean).join(", ")}
            </span>
          </span>
        )}
        {user?.age && <span>Age: <span className="font-medium">{user.age}</span></span>}
      </div>
    </div> {/* ← closes CardContent inner div */}
  </CardContent>
</Card>
