# Personas

## Solo traveler
Wants connection quickly. Often feels awkward starting from zero. Needs trust, activity, and ease.

## Local explorer
Lives in the city but wants fresh energy, new people, and shared activities.

## Social traveler
Already outgoing, but wants a better way to see who is free and aligned.

## Former Couchsurfing user
Values cultural exchange and authenticity. May assume Nearby Traveler is about hosting, so clarify it is about meeting and experiences.
