# Platform notes

## TikTok
- fast payoff
- avoid overexplaining
- spoken language should sound natural out loud
- strongest scripts often sound like observations, not ads

## Instagram
- cleaner and slightly more polished than TikTok
- ideal for reels, story copy, and influencer DM outreach

## Reddit
- promotion tolerance varies heavily by subreddit
- generate a value-first version when in doubt

## Landing page
- clarity first
- trust second
- differentiation third
