# Hook bank

## Loneliness / travel pain
- You can land in the most exciting city in the world and still feel alone by 8 p.m.
- Funny how booking a flight is easier than meeting one solid person.
- Ever spend hundreds to visit a city just to sit alone in a hotel room?
- You can plan the whole trip in advance, except the part that matters most: who you'll meet.
- Travel apps help you go somewhere. They don't help you belong there.

## Local discovery
- The best places in a city are usually hidden behind a person, not a map.
- Guidebooks show you places. Locals show you moments.

## Spontaneous plans
- Sometimes you don't need an itinerary. You need one cool person who says, "come with us."
- The best nights usually start two hours before they happen.
