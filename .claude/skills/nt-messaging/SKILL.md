# Nearby Traveler Messaging Voice

Write in Nearby Traveler's brand voice.

## Brand essence
Nearby Traveler is for people who want the best part of travel not to be left to chance: the people.

## Tone
- direct
- sharp
- human
- confident
- emotionally observant
- sometimes lightly witty

## Avoid
- corporate startup jargon
- cheesy inspiration
- vague "community" language
- sounding like a generic social network
- sounding like a hotel, booking engine, or tour company

## Core themes
- travel loneliness is real
- meaningful connection should be easier
- locals make cities come alive
- spontaneous plans are powerful
- trust matters

## Message hierarchy
1. State the real problem plainly.
2. Show the lived moment.
3. Introduce Nearby Traveler as the fix.
4. End with an immediate, clear CTA.

## Good examples of framing
- You didn't travel this far to be alone.
- The best part of travel is the people you meet.
- Book the trip in seconds. Meeting someone still feels impossible.
- Explore the city with people who are actually free.

## Copy rules
- Prefer short sentences.
- Use concrete images and situations.
- Make every line sound speakable.
- Cut filler.
- If writing a headline, produce 10 and pick the top 3.
- If writing body copy, lead with the strongest emotional truth.

## Output format
Default to:
1. strongest version
2. 2 alternates
3. why version 1 wins
